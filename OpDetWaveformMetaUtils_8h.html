Creation of <tt>sbn::OpDetWaveformMeta</tt> from <tt>raw::OpDetWaveform</tt>

The creation of summary objects sbn::OpDetWaveformMeta from optical detector waveforms is possible with two options:

  • one shot: call to convert a single waveform (makeOpDetWaveformMeta());
  • bulk: converter object reused for multiple conversions (sbn::OpDetWaveformMetaMaker).

For usage examples, see their respective documentation.
