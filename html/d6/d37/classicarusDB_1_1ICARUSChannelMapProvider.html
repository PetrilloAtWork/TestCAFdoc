Detailed Description

Interface to the channel mapping databases of TPC, CRT and PMT subdetectors.

Before retrieving the information, a run period (forPeriod()) or a run (forRun()) must be selected.

Returns the full TPC channel mapping.

— END - - - TPC fragment information - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

The returned mapping is an associative container, associating each board ID (boardID) to the following information (in a std::tuple):

  • [0] slot number the board is on (like getBoardSlot(boardID))
  • [1] list of channels served by the board, and their plane (like getChannelPlanePair(boardID))
